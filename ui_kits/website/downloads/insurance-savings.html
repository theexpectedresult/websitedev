Professional monitoring is the key

Many Canadian insurance providers offer discounts of 5% to 20% on home and business insurance premiums when a professionally monitored security system is in place. The key to maximizing your discount is professional, third-party monitoring through a ULC-certified monitoring centre — exactly what ER Tech Services provides.

Insurers reward systems that actively reduce risk, and the more comprehensive your protection, the greater the potential savings. Systems that combine intrusion detection, fire monitoring, and environmental sensors such as water leak and freeze detectors tend to qualify for the highest discounts.

Water damage: the claim insurers fear most

Water damage is one of the most costly insurance claims in Canada, and insurers are now recognizing water leak detection devices with reduced premiums — particularly systems that can automatically shut off water at the source.
